LaCie Mirror (1TB)

The Mirror measures 3 by 5 by 0.5 inches (HWD) and weighs about a half-pound. The bling is evident as soon as you take the drive out of its packaging, since all six faces are wrapped in silvered Corning Gorilla Glass. The mirrored surfaces are reflective enough to see yourself in, but be sure to keep the included microfiber cloth and carrying pouch close, as this drive is a real fingerprint and smudge magnet. The single USB port is the sole break in the Mirror's gleaming visage.The included ebony wood stand has two grooves milled into its surface: one to prop up the drive, and one to hold a pen or stylus. The drive also comes bundled with a fabric-covered USB cable so you can hook it up to your Mac or PC.
