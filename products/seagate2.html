Seagate Barracuda XT (3TB)

In most ways, this Barracuda XT is a typical 3.5-inch hard drive: It spins at 7,200 rpm (still the desktop standard) has 64MB of cache, and supports the new 6-Gbps SATA III technology for top-speed data transfers. But to take advantage of its full extra-large capacity, you need to deal with the logical block addressing issue that occurs with hard drives over 2.19TB.
