LaCie Little Big Disk Thunderbolt 2

The LaCie Little Big Disk Thunderbolt 2 ($1,299, 1TB) is the fastest external storage drive we've ever reviewed. Granted, it uses the Thunderbolt 2 interface, which is rare on everything except professional Windows workstations and Apple Macs (MacBook Pro and Mac Pro). The faster interface lets users transfer and actively work on data at more than 1,000 MBps. This is enough to transfer hundreds of gigabytes in seconds, or work on multiple HD and 4k UHD video streams simultaneously. If you need to transfer a plethora of files posthaste, or deal with very short deadlines for your video projects, then you need the Little Big Disk Thunderbolt 2. It's our new Editors' Choice for desktop-class external hard drives.
