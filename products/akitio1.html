Akitio ThunderGo (1TB)

The 1TB Akitio ThunderGo is a portable external hard drive with both Thunderbolt and USB 3.0 interfaces. It's a competent choice if you need to transfer your work between Apple and Windows PCs often, since most current Macs have a Thunderbolt interface, and almost every PC and Mac has USB 3.0. While you can certainly format any external drive to work with either a Mac or a Windows PC, the Akitio's Thunderbolt and USB 3.0 connectivity options give you more flexibility, for when, say, both USB ports are already occupied on your Apple MacBook Air, or if you have a Thunderbolt port on your PC. However, other portable drives are more attractive for their speed, more rugged exteriors, or better value. Our Editors' Choice for portable hard drives, the 4TB Seagate Backup Plus Portable Drive offers four times the capacity for $90 less.
