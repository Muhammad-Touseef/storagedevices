ADATA Premier Pro SP900 M.2 SATA (256GB)

It uses the same circuitry as a 2.5-inch SSD version of the Premier Pro SP900; the main difference is that the newer drive is more compact, and made for upgrades on desktop or laptop systems that have the M.2 SATA interface on their motherboards.
